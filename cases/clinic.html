A medium-sized private clinic in Douala manages appointments, patient records, medication inventory and billing through disconnected spreadsheets and handwritten registers.


This fragmented environment limits operational visibility, increases administrative workload and creates delays in patient care delivery.


Management lacks reliable indicators to monitor patient flows, stock consumption, consultation activities and financial performance, making informed decision-making difficult.
