SmartFlow Distribution

Inventory optimization, order automation and business intelligence for a growing food distribution company.

Tools

Excel, Power BI, Power Query and BPMN used to streamline distribution processes.

Duration

Approximately three weeks including analysis, modelling and dashboard creation.

Complexity

Medium-high complexity involving inventory, finance and logistics integration.

Context

A medium-sized food distribution company inspired by real operating environments in Cameroon imports packaged food products and supplies wholesalers, retailers and supermarkets in Douala and Yaoundé.


Inventory records, customer orders, sales reports and receivables are currently managed through disconnected Excel files maintained by different departments.


This fragmented organization creates inventory discrepancies, delays in order processing, difficulties in monitoring customer payments and limited visibility into business profitability and product performance.
