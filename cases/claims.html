SmartFlow Transport Claims

Claim management system for an interurban ticketing platform to improve passenger experience and operational reliability.

Context

Ticketing app without structured post-booking complaint handling.

Duration

Concept design and process modeling (BPMN + case study).

Complexity

Multi-actor workflow involving passengers, operators and support team.

Context

A digital interurban transport startup allows users to book and pay bus tickets via a mobile application. However, once the ticket is issued, there is no structured system to handle service issues.


Passengers rely on informal channels such as calls or messaging apps to report problems, leading to inconsistent handling and no traceability.


This creates operational blind spots and weakens trust between passengers and transport operators.
